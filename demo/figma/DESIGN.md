# Overview

This homepage is a long-scroll marketing website composed of multiple clearly separated content bands.

The visual language is restrained and product-centric.

Large areas of whitespace are used to isolate sections rather than relying on borders, shadows, or heavy backgrounds.

The homepage alternates between:

- Product demonstrations
- Social proof
- Customer quotes
- Feature showcases
- Community work galleries
- Template marketplace previews
- Footer navigation

The design system feels closer to an editorial publication than a traditional SaaS landing page.

Visual attention is directed through:

- Large typography
- Strong image blocks
- Generous spacing
- Sparse use of accent colors

Most of the visual weight comes from imagery rather than interface chrome.

# Do / Don't

## Do

- Preserve large whitespace
- Use imagery as primary storytelling device
- Keep typography simple
- Maintain section rhythm
- Keep navigation lightweight
- Use background changes sparingly

---

## Don't

- Add heavy shadows
- Add excessive gradients
- Overuse accent colors
- Introduce dense borders
- Compress section spacing
- Replace screenshots with illustrations
- Introduce highly decorative typography

---

# Iteration Guide

When extending this design:

1. Preserve whitespace before adding new visuals.
2. Keep imagery larger than supporting UI.
3. Maintain left-aligned editorial content blocks.
4. Use neutral surfaces first.
5. Reserve color for artwork and content.
6. Continue alternating visual density between sections.
7. Avoid turning sections into card-heavy SaaS layouts.
8. Preserve gallery-style storytelling.
